What makes My Maharaj different

Not just a recipe app

A complete meal planning system built around how Indian families actually eat — in communities, with festivals, with cooks, with health conditions.

01
Your cook gets the plan in their language
Open a private link on any phone. My Maharaj reads out today’s dishes in the cook’s own language — Hindi, Marathi, Kannada, Malayalam. No app download needed.
02
Health-aware from the start
Diabetic, PCOS, hypertension, cholesterol, anaemia — set it once. My Maharaj filters every dish across the week automatically. Low GI, low sodium, no maida.
03
Festival and fasting calendar built in
Navratri, Ekadashi, Ganesh Chaturthi, Eid, Bihu, Onam — My Maharaj knows every Indian festival. Fasting days get fasting dishes automatically.
04
You are always in control
Tap the pencil on any dish. See alternatives instantly — no AI credit used. Tap More for more. Ask My Maharaj when you want something completely different.
